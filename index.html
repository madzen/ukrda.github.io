What is this site?

The UKRDA occasionally undertakes maintenance operations in order to provide stable and secure services to our users.
When such operations occur (or in the event of any unforseen circumstances) and our users are affected, this site will be updated to reflect what's going on.
If you are experiencing any difficulties accessing the tools listed above and you do not see any information posted, please get in touch with tech@ukrda.org.uk so we can investigate.
