What is this site?

The UKRDA occasionally undergoes maintenance operations in order to provide a stable and secure service for our users.
When such operations occur (or in the event of any unforseen circumstances) and our service is affected, this site will be updated to reflect what's going on.
If you are experiencing any difficulties connecting to any of the above services and you do not see any information posted, please get in touch with tech@ukrda.org.uk and we will investigate.
